Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our home automation system

How difficult is it to install the system?

Our system is designed for easy installation. Most components are plug-and-play, and our mobile app will guide you through the setup process. For more complex installations, we offer professional installation services.

Is my data secure with your system?

Yes, we take security seriously. All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. We use industry-standard security protocols and regularly update our systems to protect against emerging threats.

What happens if my internet goes down?

Your automated routines will continue to work locally since they're stored on the hub. However, remote access via the app will be unavailable until internet connection is restored.

Can I integrate with other smart home devices?

Yes, our system supports integration with popular smart home platforms like Amazon Alexa, Google Assistant, and Apple HomeKit, as well as many third-party devices using standard protocols like Zigbee and Z-Wave.

What kind of warranty do you offer?

All our hardware comes with a 2-year warranty. Our software and cloud services are covered by a 99.9% uptime guarantee with 24/7 monitoring and support.
